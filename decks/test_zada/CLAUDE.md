# Zada, Hedron Grinder — Mono-Red Storm (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish Budget Commander by Tomer Abramovici

## Commander
Zada, Hedron Grinder

## Bracket
2-3 (Core to Upgraded)

## Theme
Mono-red storm. Build a board of tokens, cast Zada, then chain cantrips that copy to every creature you control. A single Expedite becomes "draw 5 cards" with 5 creatures out. Battle Hymn generates massive mana. One explosive turn wins.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Deploy token makers (Dragon Fodder, Krenko's Command, Hordeling Outburst, Mogg War Marshal)
2. T4: Cast Zada (4 mana, 3/3)
3. T5+: Chain cantrips targeting Zada — each copies to every creature. Draw 20+ cards, make 30+ mana, pump team, swing for lethal
4. Alternative: Mirrorwing Dragon as backup Zada

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 mountains + token producer + cantrip. You need board before Zada.
- KEEP: 2 mountains + Sol Ring/Iron Myr + token maker
- MULLIGAN: No token producers. All cantrips but no creatures. 1 land.
- The deck does NOTHING without creatures on board when Zada resolves

## Win Conditions
- Storm turn: pump entire team with Brute Force/Titan's Strength copies, swing for 40+ damage
- Temur Battle Rage copied = double strike on everything
- Hellrider + massive token army = drain on attack
- Combat Celebrant for extra combats

## Known Weakness
- Removal on Zada mid-combo is devastating
- Board wipes before the storm turn
- Does nothing until the big turn — very vulnerable to aggro
