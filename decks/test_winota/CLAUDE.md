# Winota, Joiner of Forces — Boros Aggro/Stax (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish user-submitted Bracket 3 deck ($309)

## Commander
Winota, Joiner of Forces

## Bracket
3 (Upgraded — 2 Game Changers: Drannith Magistrate, Teferi's Protection)

## Theme
Boros aggro with stax elements. Non-human creatures attack, triggering Winota to cheat human creatures from the top of the library into play attacking. Stax pieces (Thalia, Rule of Law, Drannith Magistrate) slow opponents while Winota generates explosive board presence.

## Strategy
1. T1-2: Deploy cheap non-human creatures (Ornithopter, Gingerbrute, Bomat Courier, Loyal Apprentice)
2. T3-4: Cast Winota, attack with non-humans. Each trigger digs 6 deep for a human to put into play attacking.
3. T5+: Chain attack triggers — each Winota trigger can find Blade Historian, Angrath's Marauders, Grand Abolisher. Kiki-Jiki + Felidar Guardian = infinite combo backup.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 2-3 lands + 2 cheap non-human creatures. Winota needs attackers to trigger.
- KEEP: Sol Ring + land + any non-human creature
- MULLIGAN: All humans (Winota needs non-humans to trigger). No creatures at all. 1 land.

## Win Conditions
- Winota triggers flooding the board with powerful humans in a single combat
- Kiki-Jiki + Felidar Guardian/Village Bell-Ringer = infinite tokens
- Angrath's Marauders doubling all damage
- Stax lock (Drannith Magistrate + Rule of Law) while beating down

## Known Weakness
- Winota is a removal magnet — 4 mana commander that needs to attack to trigger
- Board wipes before Winota attacks are devastating
- Needs both non-humans (to trigger) and humans (to find) — can draw the wrong half
