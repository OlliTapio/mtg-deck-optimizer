# Valgavoth, Harrower of Souls — Rakdos Punishment (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Duskmourn: House of Horror Commander Precon "Endless Punishment" (2024)

## Commander
Valgavoth, Harrower of Souls (partner with The Lord of Pain)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Rakdos group slug / punishment. Punish opponents for existing — drawing cards, tapping lands, casting spells, having creatures. Valgavoth grows with +1/+1 counters and draws cards whenever opponents lose life. Every pain effect feeds the engine.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Deploy mana rocks and early punishers (Blood Seeker, Harsh Mentor, Kederekt Parasite)
2. T4-5: Cast Valgavoth, begin accumulating counters from opponent life loss
3. T6+: Stack punishment effects (Fate Unraveler, Spiteful Visions, Mogis). Every opponent action costs life, feeds Valgavoth, draws you cards.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands + mana rock + early punisher creature + any removal
- KEEP: Sol Ring + 2 lands + Valgavoth castable + any card
- MULLIGAN: No early punishers. All expensive cards. One land.

## Win Conditions
- Valgavoth grows huge from constant life loss triggers, swings for commander damage
- Accumulated drain from Blood Artist / Falkenrath Noble / Bastion of Remembrance
- Gray Merchant of Asphodel for massive drain
- Kaervek the Merciless punishes every spell cast
- Massacre Wurm after opponents have small creatures = massive drain

## Known Weakness
- Punishment effects hurt you too (Spiteful Visions, Stormfist Crusader)
- No lifegain means you race your own cards
- Valgavoth is expensive (5 mana) and draws removal
- Weak to enchantment removal (many punishers are enchantments)
