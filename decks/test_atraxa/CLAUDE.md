# Atraxa, Praetors' Voice — 4C Infect/Proliferate (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: EDHREC Average Decklist (community aggregate)

## Commander
Atraxa, Praetors' Voice

## Bracket
4 (Optimized)

## Theme
Four-color infect/proliferate. Use Atraxa's built-in proliferate to accelerate poison counters, loyalty counters, and experience counters. Premium manabase with fetchlands and shocklands. Multiple Game Changers.

## Game Changers in Deck
- Cyclonic Rift
- Rhystic Study
- Smothering Tithe
- Narset, Parter of Veils
- Teferi's Protection
(5 Game Changers — exceeds bracket 3 limit of 3)

## Strategy
1. T1-2: Ramp with mana rocks and dorks (Birds of Paradise, Sol Ring, Arcane Signet)
2. T3-4: Cast Atraxa, begin proliferating. Deploy infect creatures (Blighted Agent, Plague Stinger)
3. T5+: Proliferate poison counters to 10. Use Contagion Engine and Inexorable Tide for multiple proliferates per turn.
4. Alternative: Planeswalker ultimates via proliferate (Vraska, Tamiyo)

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands (color-diverse) + ramp + infect creature or Atraxa castable by T4
- KEEP: Sol Ring + 2 lands + any 2 spells
- MULLIGAN: Mono-color hand in a 4-color deck. No ramp and no early plays. All infect but no mana.

## Win Conditions
- Poison counters via infect creatures + Atraxa proliferate (10 poison = dead)
- Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon for commander damage via infect
- Planeswalker ultimates (Vraska's emblem, Tamiyo's emblem)
- Overwhelming value from Rhystic Study + Smothering Tithe

## Known Weakness
- 4 colors requires careful mana management
- Infect draws hate — opponents will team up against you
- Vulnerable to early aggro before defenses are established
- Graveyard hate doesn't matter much, but enchantment/artifact removal hurts
