# Tidus, Yuna's Guardian — Bant Counters (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Final Fantasy Commander Precon "Counter Blitz" (FFX, 2025)

## Commander
Tidus, Yuna's Guardian (partner with Yuna, Grand Summoner)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Bant +1/+1 counter manipulation. Tidus moves counters between creatures, turning combat math into a dynamic puzzle. Proliferate and counter doublers (Hardened Scales) accelerate the strategy.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Ramp (Farseek, Three Visits, Sol Ring) and deploy counter generators
2. T4: Cast Tidus, begin moving and proliferating counters
3. T5+: Hardened Scales + Inexorable Tide + proliferate = exponential counter growth. Walking Ballista as a flexible win condition.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands + ramp + any counter-matters creature
- KEEP: 2 lands + Sol Ring + Hardened Scales or Fight Rigging
- MULLIGAN: No green mana for ramp. No early counter generators.

## Win Conditions
- Walking Ballista with massive counters for direct damage
- Chasm Skulker death trigger spawning huge squid army
- Overwhelming board of creatures with 5+ counters each
- Sin, Unending Cataclysm as FF-themed finisher
- Bane of Progress destroying all artifacts/enchantments entering as huge creature
