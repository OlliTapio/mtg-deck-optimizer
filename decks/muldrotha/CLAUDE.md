# Muldrotha, the Gravetide

## Commander
Muldrotha, the Gravetide ({3}{B}{G}{U}, 6/6 Elemental Avatar) — during each of your turns, you may play a land and cast a permanent spell of each permanent type from your graveyard. This means up to one creature, one artifact, one enchantment, one planeswalker, and one land per turn from the graveyard.

## Bracket 3 (Upgraded)
- **Game Changers used: 0 of 3** — no Game Changers in the list
- Altar of Dementia is powerful but not on the Game Changers list
- No two-card infinite combos exist in this build
- No mass land denial
- Expected game length: 7-9 turns (mill takes time to fill graveyards, then big finishers close it out)

## Theme and Design Principles

### Mill + Reanimate
The deck mills everyone (including itself), then uses graveyards as a shared card pool. Self-mill fuels Muldrotha's ability to replay permanents. Opponent mill feeds reanimation targets and grows payoffs like Consuming Aberration and Syr Konrad.

### Permanent-Heavy Construction
Almost all interaction is permanent-based so Muldrotha can replay it from the graveyard:
- **Creature removal:** Ravenous Chupacabra, Plaguecrafter, Acidic Slime (ETB effects, replayable)
- **Enchantment removal:** Seal of Doom, Seal of Primordium, Song of the Dryads, Kenrith's Transformation, Lignify (sacrifice/enchant-based, replayable)
- **Artifact removal:** Executioner's Capsule, Nihil Spellbomb (sacrifice-based, replayable)
- Only 5 instants (3 counters + Putrefy + Contaminated Drink) and 5 sorceries kept for maximum Muldrotha value

### Key Engine Interactions
- **Altar of Dementia + any creature:** sacrifice to mill, then replay with Muldrotha or reanimate
- **Animate Dead / Necromancy:** enchantment-based reanimation, Muldrotha replays these each turn as the enchantment slot
- **Syr Konrad + mass mill:** drains opponents whenever creatures hit graveyards or leave them
- **The Scarab God:** exiles creatures from any graveyard as 4/4 zombies, scries and drains based on zombie count
- **Spore Frog:** sacrifice to fog, replay with Muldrotha every turn for near-immortality
- **Seal cycle (Doom, Primordium):** sacrifice for removal, replay with Muldrotha each turn

### Win Conditions
1. **Rise of the Dark Realms (9 CMC):** steal all creatures from all graveyards after milling everyone
2. **Living Death:** swap boards with graveyards when yours is full and theirs aren't
3. **Syr Konrad + mass mill:** passive drain damage from graveyard movement
4. **Consuming Aberration:** grows massive from opponent mill, mill more on each spell cast
5. **Altar of Dementia loops:** sacrifice big creatures to mill, reanimate, repeat

### Rad Counter Sub-theme
Fallout cards (Infesting Radroach, Glowing One, Undead Alchemist) give opponents rad counters, which mill on their upkeep. This is free, repeatable mill that doesn't require our resources.

## Known Issues
- **Only 1 board wipe** (Living Death, which doubles as a win condition). The deck relies on targeted permanent-based removal instead. Could be a weakness against wide token strategies.
- **Graveyard hate vulnerability:** Bojuka Bog and Nihil Spellbomb provide our own graveyard hate, but if opponents exile our graveyard, the deck loses its main engine. No protection like Perpetual Timepiece's shuffle ability or Tamiyo's static covers this partially.
- **Slow start possible:** 6 CMC commander means turns 1-5 are setup. The deck needs ramp and early mill to function.
- **High tapland count:** 12 lands enter tapped. Necessary for budget and color fixing but can slow the deck.
- **No haste enablers:** reanimated creatures can't attack immediately.
- **Reanimate (sorcery) is a nonbo with Muldrotha** — can't replay it from graveyard with Muldrotha since it's a sorcery, not a permanent. Included anyway for raw power at 1 mana.

## Mulligan Strategy

### Goal
Cast Muldrotha on turn 4-5 with some graveyard fuel already in place. Muldrotha costs {3}{B}{G}{U} — needs all three colors plus 3 generic.

### Auto-Keep
- 3+ lands including at least 2 colors + a 2-CMC mana rock (Talisman, Sol Ring). This lets you cast Muldrotha turn 4-5.
- 3+ lands including all 3 colors + any mill engine (Hedron Crab, Stitcher's Supplier, Codex Shredder). You'll have fuel ready when Muldrotha hits.
- 2 lands + Sol Ring + a color source in hand. Sol Ring alone enables turn 4 Muldrotha.

### Good Keep
- 3-4 lands with 2+ colors and any action (ramp creature, mill creature, removal). Even without Muldrotha acceleration, natural curve into turn 6 Muldrotha is fine.
- 2 lands + 2 ramp pieces (talismans, Wayfarer's Bauble, ramp creatures). Risk of missing land drop 3, but ramp covers it.
- 4+ lands with a reanimation target and Animate Dead/Necromancy. Can shortcut to a big play without Muldrotha.

### Mulligan
- 1 or fewer lands — always mulligan. Even with Sol Ring, too risky.
- No green or black source — can't cast most of the deck. Need at least one of each in first few turns.
- All lands, no action — 5+ lands with nothing to do is a slow death.
- All expensive cards (5+ CMC) with no ramp — won't survive to cast anything meaningful.
- Hand full of counters/instants with no permanents — Muldrotha can't replay these.

### Priority Order for Opening Hand
1. **Lands producing B and G** (non-negotiable — most pips in the deck)
2. **2-CMC mana rock** (Sol Ring, Talisman cycle — enables turn 4-5 Muldrotha)
3. **Early mill** (Hedron Crab, Stitcher's Supplier, Codex Shredder — fuel the graveyard before Muldrotha lands)
4. **Ramp creature** (Diligent Farmhand, Wood Elves, Farhaven Elf — ramp + goes to graveyard for Muldrotha replay value)
5. **Early removal** (Seal of Doom, Caustic Caterpillar, Executioner's Capsule — protect yourself in early game, replay later)
6. **U source** (need it for Muldrotha, but B and G are higher priority since more pips)

### Dream Curves

**Curve A — Fast Muldrotha:**
- T1: Land, Sol Ring
- T2: Land, Talisman of Resilience + Hedron Crab
- T3: Land (Hedron Crab mills 3), Sidisi, Brood Tyrant
- T4: Land (Hedron Crab mills 3), Muldrotha (7 mana available)
- T5: Play land from graveyard (Hedron Crab mills 3), replay Stitcher's Supplier from GY, replay Seal of Doom from GY, replay any artifact from GY — 4 permanent types replayed

**Curve B — Mill into Reanimate:**
- T1: Land, Stitcher's Supplier (mills 3)
- T2: Land, Codex Shredder (mill starts), Mire Triton (mills 2)
- T3: Land, Animate Dead on something good from any graveyard
- T4: Land, Talisman + Altar of Dementia
- T5: Muldrotha, start the engine

**Curve C — Steady Build:**
- T1: Land, Wayfarer's Bauble
- T2: Land, crack Bauble for basic, Sultai Ascendancy
- T3: Land, Wood Elves (fetch Forest)
- T4: Land, Plaguecrafter + Seal of Primordium
- T5: Muldrotha (5 lands + ramp from Bauble)
- T6: Replay Wayfarer's Bauble (artifact), Plaguecrafter (creature), any enchantment — full Muldrotha value

## Game Simulation Findings (2026-03-18)

### Performance: 3 LLM games, 1 win (won without Ureni in pod, eliminated T6 in another)
- Won via Consuming Aberration as 80/80 fueled by everyone's graveyards
- Syr Konrad + Blasphemous Act drained 12 life from each opponent in one board wipe
- Spore Frog replayed every turn = repeatable fog, bought critical time
- Graveyard engine is unstoppable once online, but slow to establish (T6-7 for Muldrotha)

### MVP Cards
- **Syr Konrad** — every creature dying or being milled drained all opponents
- **Consuming Aberration** — became 60-80/80 as graveyards filled from all sources
- **Seal of Doom** — killed Ureni at instant speed in a crucial political play

### Underperformers
- **Seal of Primordium** — no good artifact/enchantment targets in most games
- **Llanowar Wastes** — painland with NO land types caused Drowned Catacomb and Woodland Cemetery to enter tapped, losing 2 turns of tempo

### Improvement Priorities
1. **Replace Llanowar Wastes with Overgrown Tomb** (or budget typed dual) — check lands need typed lands
2. Add haste enabler (Lightning Greaves/Swiftfoot Boots) — Consuming Aberration as 80/80 that can't attack for a turn cycle gives opponents time to answer
3. Add more self-mill T1-2 (Stitcher's Supplier is great, consider Hedron Crab if not already included)
4. Consider a secondary win condition beyond combat (Altar of Dementia mill-out)

### Round 3 Findings (seed 6931)
- Eliminated T6 by Brion stealing+flinging Yuma at Muldrotha (6 damage at 4 life)
- Three tapped lands in opener = no plays until T3. Needs untapped colored sources
- Brigid's aggro beat Muldrotha from 41 to 4 in 4 turns — needs early blockers badly
- Living Death drawn T6 but couldn't cast (needed 5 mana, had 4) — one turn too slow
- Add Baleful Strix or similar cheap blocker that replaces itself
