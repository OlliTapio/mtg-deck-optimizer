# Edric, Spymaster of Trest — Simic Tempo (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish Budget Commander by Tomer Abramovici (TappedOut budget version)

## Commander
Edric, Spymaster of Trest

## Bracket
2 (Core)

## Theme
Evasion-based tempo. Flood the board with cheap unblockable creatures, draw tons of cards off Edric's combat trigger, keep opponents off-balance with bounce and countermagic.

## Strategy
1. T1-2: Deploy 1-CMC evasive creatures (Triton Shorestalker, Cloud Sprite, Zephyr Sprite, Metathran Soldier)
2. T3: Cast Edric, attack with everything, draw 3-5 cards
3. T4+: Keep deploying threats and drawing cards. Use bounce (Aetherize, Sigil of Sleep) to stay ahead.
4. Close with Psychosis Crawler or overwhelming board presence

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 2-3 evasive creatures + 2-3 lands (including green + blue). Edric by T3 is the goal.
- KEEP: 1 creature + Edric + Coastal Piracy/Bident of Thassa + 3 lands
- MULLIGAN: No creatures. Only big creatures. No blue mana.
- The deck NEEDS early board presence before Edric resolves

## Win Conditions
- Overwhelming card advantage from Edric triggers leading to board dominance
- Psychosis Crawler + massive card draw = direct damage
- Stormtide Leviathan + evasive army
- Scourge of Fleets to bounce opponent boards
