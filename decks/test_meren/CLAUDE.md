# Meren of Clan Nel Toth — Golgari Graveyard Value (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish Budget EDH ($82)

## Commander
Meren of Clan Nel Toth

## Bracket
3 (Upgraded)

## Theme
Graveyard-based value engine. Sacrifice creatures to accumulate experience counters on Meren, then reanimate increasingly powerful threats each end step. Self-mill to fill the graveyard, then recur the best pieces repeatedly.

## Strategy
1. T1-2: Ramp (Llanowar Elves, Sakura-Tribe Elder) and self-mill (Satyr Wayfinder, Mulch)
2. T3-4: Cast Meren, start sacrificing creatures for experience counters
3. T5+: Reanimate powerful creatures each end step (Terastodon, Gray Merchant of Asphodel, Butcher of Malakir)
4. Lock: Fleshbag Marauder / Plaguecrafter recurring every turn forces opponents to sacrifice

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 2-3 lands (including green) + ramp creature + self-mill or sac outlet
- KEEP: Meren castable by T4 + Sakura-Tribe Elder or Spore Frog
- MULLIGAN: No green mana. All expensive cards. No early plays.

## Win Conditions
- Gray Merchant of Asphodel recurring every turn for massive drain
- Jarad, Golgari Lich Lord + big creature flung at opponents
- Syr Konrad with repeated graveyard recursion for incremental damage
- Attrition lock: recurring edict creatures until opponents have nothing

## Known Weakness
- Graveyard hate (Rest in Peace, Bojuka Bog) shuts down the entire strategy
- Meren needs to survive AND needs experience counters — slow start if removed early
- Weak to flying/evasive aggro in the early game
