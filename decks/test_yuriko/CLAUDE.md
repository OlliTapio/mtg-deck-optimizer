# Yuriko, the Tiger's Shadow — Dimir Ninja Aggro (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish Budget Yuriko ($50)

## Commander
Yuriko, the Tiger's Shadow

## Bracket
3 (Upgraded)

## Theme
Dimir ninja tribal with evasive creatures and ninjutsu. Yuriko's commander ninjutsu bypasses commander tax entirely — she costs UB every time. Each ninja hitting an opponent draws a card and deals damage equal to its CMC. High-CMC cards in the deck maximize Yuriko's damage trigger.

## Strategy
1. T1: Deploy a cheap evasive creature (Changeling Outcast, Mist-Cloaked Herald, Ornithopter)
2. T2: Ninjutsu Yuriko into play off the unblocked creature. Draw a card, deal damage.
3. T3+: Keep ninjutsuing — bounce creatures back, replay them, ninjutsu again. Each hit draws + damages.
4. Late game: High-CMC cards (Silent-Blade Oni, Ever After, Insidious Will) flipped by Yuriko deal 5-7 damage per trigger.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 1-2 cheap evasive creatures + 2 lands (including U and B). Yuriko is always available via ninjutsu.
- KEEP: Changeling Outcast + any 2 lands. That's enough to start the Yuriko engine T2.
- MULLIGAN: No evasive creatures. No blue mana. All expensive cards with no cheap enablers.

## Win Conditions
- Yuriko triggers dealing 3-7 damage per hit to ALL opponents simultaneously
- Multiple ninjas connecting = 15+ damage per combat to the table
- Silent-Blade Oni steals spells from opponents' hands
- Fallen Shinobi exiles and casts opponents' cards for free

## Known Weakness
- Needs evasive creatures to enable ninjutsu — board wipes are painful
- Low creature power means combat damage alone is slow
- Yuriko draws hate as she damages ALL opponents equally
- Limited to budget interaction — no Force of Will, no Cyclonic Rift
