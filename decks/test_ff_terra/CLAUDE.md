# Terra, Herald of Hope — Mardu Reanimator (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Final Fantasy Commander Precon "Revival Trance" (FFVI, 2025)

## Commander
Terra, Herald of Hope (partner with Celes, Rune Knight)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Mardu reanimator with graveyard value. Mill yourself to fill the graveyard, then reanimate powerful creatures. Terra's Trance ability mills 2 and gives her flying, and her combat damage trigger reanimates creatures with power 3 or less.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Self-mill (Stitcher's Supplier, Millikin) and ramp (mana rocks)
2. T4-5: Cast Terra, attack to reanimate small value creatures. Use Reanimate/Stitch Together for big targets.
3. T6+: Rise of the Dark Realms / Sepulchral Primordial for massive graveyard payoff. Pitiless Plunderer for treasure generation.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands + self-mill creature + ramp or reanimation spell
- KEEP: 2 lands + Sol Ring + any early play
- MULLIGAN: No mana fixing in 3 colors. No early plays. All reanimation targets but no way to mill or reanimate them.

## Win Conditions
- Rise of the Dark Realms to reanimate everything
- Ruinous Ultimatum to devastate one opponent
- Sun Titan recurring small permanents every combat
- Sepulchral Primordial stealing from all opponents' graveyards
- Kefka, Dancing Mad (FF-specific finisher)
