# Niv-Mizzet, Parun — Izzet Spellslinger (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: EDHREC Average Budget Decklist

## Commander
Niv-Mizzet, Parun

## Bracket
3 (Upgraded — no Game Changers but powerful combo potential)

## Theme
Izzet spellslinger with combo finish. Every instant/sorcery you cast draws a card (Niv-Mizzet trigger) and pings an opponent. Curiosity on Niv-Mizzet = infinite combo (draw triggers ping, ping triggers draw). Even without the combo, the deck generates massive card advantage and controls the board with countermagic.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Deploy mana rocks, cantrip (Opt, Preordain, Ponder) to set up
2. T4-6: Cast Niv-Mizzet (costs UUURRR — color-intensive!). Begin drawing extra cards.
3. T7+: Chain spells for massive triggers. Curiosity or Tandem Lookout = instant win combo. Thousand-Year Storm for explosive spell chains.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3+ lands with both blue AND red mana + mana rock + cantrip. Niv costs UUURRR so color fixing is critical.
- KEEP: Sol Ring + 2 lands (one of each color) + any spells
- MULLIGAN: All one color. No ramp for 6-mana commander. No blue mana.
- Niv-Mizzet can't be countered, so you don't need protection — just mana.

## Win Conditions
- Curiosity / Tandem Lookout on Niv-Mizzet = infinite draw + ping (instant kill)
- Guttersnipe + Electrostatic Field + spell chain = massive burn
- Thousand-Year Storm copying spells exponentially
- Psychosis Crawler + mass card draw
- Talrand / Young Pyromancer / Murmuring Mystic token armies from spell casting

## Known Weakness
- UUURRR casting cost means Niv often comes down T6-7
- Creature removal on Niv-Mizzet before Curiosity attaches stops the combo
- Very few creatures means vulnerable to early aggro
- Limited board wipes (Chain Reaction only)
