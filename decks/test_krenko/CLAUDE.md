# Krenko, Mob Boss — Mono-Red Goblins (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish Budget Commander ($54)

## Commander
Krenko, Mob Boss

## Bracket
3 (Upgraded)

## Theme
Goblin tribal token swarm. Krenko's activated ability doubles your goblin count each turn. With haste enablers and untap effects (Thornbite Staff, Sword of the Paruns), Krenko can generate hundreds of tokens in a single turn.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Deploy cheap goblins (Skirk Prospector, Goblin Sledder, Foundry Street Denizen)
2. T4: Cast Krenko, ideally with haste (Goblin Warchief, Goblin Chieftain, Swiftfoot Boots)
3. T5+: Tap Krenko each turn to double goblins. Use Thornbite Staff for infinite untaps with a sac outlet.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 mountains + Goblin Warchief or Swiftfoot Boots + 1-2 goblins. Haste for Krenko is critical.
- KEEP: 2 mountains + Sol Ring + any goblin + any card
- MULLIGAN: No goblins. No way to give Krenko haste. 5+ mana hands.

## Win Conditions
- Goblin Bombardment + infinite tokens = infinite damage
- Impact Tremors + mass token generation = drain
- Massive goblin army + Goblin Chieftain/Hellrider swing
- Throne of the God-Pharaoh after tapping all goblins
- Burn at the Stake with 20+ goblins

## Known Weakness
- Board wipes devastate the token army
- Krenko must survive and tap — instant-speed removal in response to tap is backbreaking
- No card draw without Skullclamp or Outpost Siege
