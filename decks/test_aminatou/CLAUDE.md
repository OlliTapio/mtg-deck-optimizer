# Aminatou, Veil Piercer — Esper Enchantments/Miracle (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Duskmourn: House of Horror Commander Precon "Miracle Worker" (2024)

## Commander
Aminatou, Veil Piercer (partner with The Master of Keys)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Esper enchantment-matters with miracle synergy. Aminatou grants miracle to enchantments in hand (reduced by 4 mana). Top-deck manipulation (Ponder, Brainstorm, Portent) to set up miracle casts. Enchantment payoffs generate tokens and value.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Deploy mana rocks and top-deck manipulation (Ponder, Brainstorm, Portent)
2. T4-5: Cast Aminatou, start miracling enchantments for cheap
3. T6+: Sigil of the Empty Throne + enchantment spam = angel army. Sphere of Safety for defense.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands + mana rock + top-deck manipulation spell
- KEEP: Sol Ring + 2 lands + Aminatou castable + any enchantment
- MULLIGAN: No blue mana. No early plays. All expensive enchantments with no way to miracle them.

## Win Conditions
- Sigil of the Empty Throne: each enchantment = 4/4 angel token
- Entreat the Angels miracled for a massive angel army
- Shark Typhoon tokens from casting noncreature spells
- Archetype of Imagination makes everything unblockable

## Known Weakness
- Slow to develop — needs enchantments AND top-deck manipulation online
- Vulnerable to enchantment removal (Bane of Progress)
- Limited creature count means weak early board
- Three-color mana in a precon manabase is inconsistent
