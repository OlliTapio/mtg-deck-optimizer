# Mothman Game Report — 10 LLM Games vs Test Opponents

## Record: 0-10 (0% win rate)
- Average elimination turn: 7.0
- Gets focused as primary target in every game

## Critical Finding
Rad counters are too slow. They deal 1-3 damage/turn while opponents deal 5-12 combat damage/turn. The deck needs 4 turns of setup before the engine starts. Aggro decks have 3-6 creatures by then.

## Worst Matchups
- Go-wide aggro (Krenko, Zada, Teysa): wide boards swing for 7-12 per turn
- Atraxa: same theme but better card quality, outpaces Mothman
- Evasive aggro (Edric, Yuriko): unblockable chip damage is constant

## MVP Cards
- Incubation Druid (best ramp, enabled T3-4 Mothman)
- Nature's Lore / Farseek (color fixing ramp)
- Nuclear Fallout (only board wipe, bought extra turns)
- Defiler of Vigor (when cast, immediately threatening)

## Underperformers
- Psychic Corrosion (mill enchantment, no board impact)
- Guardian Project (4-mana draw engine, too slow when losing)
- Simic Ascendancy (20 counters is unreachable under pressure)
- Armorcraft Judge (5 CMC 3/3 with no evasion)
- Tato Farmer (landfall too slow)

## Recommended Changes
1. Add early blockers/deterrents: Propaganda/Elephant Grass effects, deathtouch creatures
2. Add 3-4 board wipes (Toxic Deluge, Black Sun's Zenith synergizes with -1/-1 counters)
3. Cut win-more cards (Guardian Project, Armorcraft Judge, Simic Ascendancy)
4. More cheap instant removal (Go for the Throat, Reality Shift, Rapid Hybridization)
5. Pivot strategy: lean into +1/+1 counter BEATDOWN with evasion, treat rad counters as incidental
6. Add rattlesnake effects to redirect aggression to other players
