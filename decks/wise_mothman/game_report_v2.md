## Strategy Lessons (validated)
1. Commander damage IS the primary win — 4/4 wins via flying 21+ damage
2. Deploy counter doubler BEFORE Mothman — dramatically faster kills
3. Radstag is the best board-presence creature (evolve + token copy)
4. Rad counters are incidental but add 8-15 life loss over a full game
5. Swiftfoot Boots critical once Mothman is 10/10+
