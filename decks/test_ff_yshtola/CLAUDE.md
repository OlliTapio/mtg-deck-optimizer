# Y'shtola, Night's Blessed — Esper Control (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Final Fantasy Commander Precon "Scions & Spellcraft" (FFXIV, 2025)

## Commander
Y'shtola, Night's Blessed (partner with G'raha Tia, Scion Reborn)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Esper control with noncreature spell focus. Y'shtola rewards casting instants and sorceries with value. Heavy on board wipes, targeted removal, and card draw. Wins through controlling the game and deploying finishers.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Ramp with artifacts (16 artifacts in deck), deploy Propaganda/Authority of the Consuls for defense
2. T4-5: Cast Y'shtola, start generating value from spells. Hold up interaction.
3. T6+: Board wipe repeatedly (Cleansing Nova, Crux of Fate, Final Judgment). Exsanguinate for massive drain. Rite of Replication for explosive finisher.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands + 2 mana rocks + any interaction
- KEEP: 2 lands + Sol Ring + any spell
- MULLIGAN: No artifacts/ramp. All creatures but no spells. Mono-color in a 3-color deck.

## Win Conditions
- Exsanguinate for lethal drain (scales with mana rocks)
- Rite of Replication kicked on Torrential Gearhulk or opponent's best creature
- Sublime Epiphany for massive tempo swing
- Torrential Gearhulk re-casting instants from graveyard
- Archmage Emeritus + spell spam for overwhelming card advantage
