# Ashling Game Report — 10 LLM Games vs Test Opponents

## Record: 0-10 (0% win rate)
- Average elimination turn: 7.2
- Killed one opponent (Yuriko in Game 7) — only kill across all 10 games
- Avg damage dealt: 10.9/game. Avg damage taken: 41.4/game

## Critical Findings
1. Evoke chain NEVER fired in any game — the core strategy never materialized
2. Commander deployed too early in 7/10 games (ignoring HOLD strategy)
3. Tapland manabase costs a full turn of development (8/10 games had tapland T1)
4. Archenemy target in all 10 games — absorbed 3x the damage dealt

## Best Game: Game 7 (seed 77777) vs Yuriko/Krenko/Meren
- Best early game: Flamebraider T2 → Ashling Rekindled T3 → Commander T4
- Shriekmaw evoke + copy killed Yuriko T7 (the only evoke-copy play that worked)
- Dealt 21 damage total before dying T8

## MVP Cards
- Horde of Notions (5/5, WUBRG reanimates Elementals from GY)
- Shriekmaw (evoke removal + Ashling copy = kill 2 creatures)
- Flamekin Harbinger (tutors the right Elemental)
- Selvala, Heart of the Wilds (mana engine)

## Underperformers
- Tri-lands (Jungle Shrine, Seaside Citadel etc.) — always entered tapped, cost full turns
- The evoke chain concept itself — never assembled in 10 games
- Realmwalker, Flock Impostor — small value, not enough to stabilize

## Recommended Changes
1. MANA BASE OVERHAUL: Replace tri-lands with untapped duals or basics + more 2-CMC ramp
2. More board wipes: need Blasphemous Act + Shatter the Sky + at least 1 more
3. More early interaction: Path to Exile, Swords to Plowshares for T1-2 defense
4. The HOLD strategy is correct but needs a human pilot — auto-pilot always rushes commander
5. Flamekin Harbinger should be a mulligan priority (tutors the evoke target)
6. The 5-color manabase is the deck's biggest weakness — consider cutting to 3-4 colors
