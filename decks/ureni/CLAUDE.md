# Ureni of the Unwritten

## Commander
Ureni of the Unwritten — {4}{G}{U}{R} 6/6 Spirit Dragon, flying, trample. On ETB or attack, look at top 8 cards — put a Dragon onto the battlefield for free.

## Bracket
3 (Upgraded) — no Game Changers, no infinite combos, no mass land denial. Power-based: Ureni's free dragon cheating + Miirym/Hellkite Courser/Scourge of the Throne can close games by turn 6-7.

## Theme
Temur (RGU) Dragon tribal. Ramp hard into Ureni, cheat massive dragons onto the battlefield with his trigger, swing with a flying army. Ureni, the Song Unending in the 99 as a separate finisher (X damage ETB where X = lands you control).

## Design Principles
- Ramp-first gameplan — Ureni costs 7, most dragons cost 5-7
- Dragon density matters — Ureni's top-8 trigger whiffs less with more dragons
- Blink/protection for Ureni re-triggers ETB (Essence Flux, Teferi's Time Twist, Last Night Together)
- Budget: no single card over €10

## Known Issues
- Blue sources light (17 sources for 28 pips) — most dual lands are RG or GU, UR is weak
- Average CMC 4.32 — very top-heavy, vulnerable to slow starts
- Only 1 recursion source (Haven of the Spirit Dragon)
- Only 3 counterspells — limited protection for the board

## Mulligan Strategy

Goal: cast Ureni by turn 5. He costs {4}{G}{U}{R} — all 3 colors required, 7 total mana.

### Auto-keep
- 3+ lands with all 3 colors (or 2 colors + fixer) and a 2 CMC ramp spell — Ureni turn 5 with ramp, or turn 4 with Sol Ring + ramp.
- 2 lands + Sol Ring + any ramp — Sol Ring turn 1, ramp turn 2, Ureni turn 4.
- 3+ lands with 2 colors + Farseek/Nature's Lore — these find typed duals to fix the missing color.

### Good keep
- 4 lands + dragon + draw engine (Elemental Bond, Garruk's Uprising, Temur Ascendancy) — natural Ureni turn 5-6, draw engine online before dragons start hitting.
- 3 lands + 2 ramp spells even without all 3 colors — double ramp finds colors and gets to 7 fast.
- 3 lands + Hellkite Courser — backup plan to cheat Ureni from command zone even if you can't hard cast him.

### Mulligan
- 1 land even with ramp — Ureni needs 7 mana, can't recover from 1 land.
- No green sources — green has the most pips (35) and all the ramp requires green.
- All dragons, no ramp — a hand of 6+ CMC cards with no way to accelerate is dead for 5 turns.
- 2+ lands but only 1 color and no fixers — can't cast Ureni or most spells.

### Priority order for opening hand
1. Green-producing land (non-negotiable — enables all ramp spells)
2. 2 CMC ramp (Farseek, Nature's Lore, Rampant Growth, Sakura-Tribe Elder) — turn 5 Ureni
3. Color fixing for U and R (Farseek/Nature's Lore find typed duals)
4. Sol Ring / Jade Orb / Carnelian Orb — fast mana toward 7
5. Draw engine (Temur Ascendancy, Elemental Bond, Garruk's Uprising) — deploy before Ureni
6. Counterspell/protection — nice to have, not a keep reason

### Dream curves
**Fast start:**
- T1: Forest, Sol Ring
- T2: Land, Farseek (find Island or Mountain dual)
- T3: Land, Temur Ascendancy
- T4: Land, Ureni — trigger finds a dragon, both have haste from Ascendancy

**Ramp start:**
- T1: Land
- T2: Land, Rampant Growth
- T3: Land, Kodama's Reach
- T4: Land, Frontier Siege (Khans mode = +2 mana)
- T5: Ureni with mana to spare
