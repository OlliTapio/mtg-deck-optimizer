# Teysa Karlov — Orzhov Aristocrats (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: MTGGoldfish Budget Commander by Tomer Abramovici

## Commander
Teysa Karlov

## Bracket
2 (Core)

## Theme
Sacrifice-based aristocrats. Flood the board with creatures that make tokens on death, sacrifice them for value via Blood Artist/Zulaport Cutthroat drain effects. Teysa doubles all death triggers, turning each sacrifice into double the drain, double the tokens, double the card draw.

## Strategy
1. Early game: Deploy cheap creatures with death triggers (Doomed Traveler, Hunted Witness, Carrier Thrall)
2. Mid game: Establish sacrifice outlets (Viscera Seer, Carrion Feeder, Yahenni) + payoffs (Blood Artist, Zulaport Cutthroat)
3. Late game: Cast Teysa to double everything, drain the table with repeated sacrifices

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 2-3 lands + sac outlet + death trigger creature + payoff (Blood Artist/Cutthroat)
- KEEP: 3 lands + Teysa castable by T4 + any 2 creatures
- MULLIGAN: No sac outlet and no payoff. No lands. All expensive cards.
- Sol Ring is always a keep

## Win Conditions
- Drain via Blood Artist / Zulaport Cutthroat / Falkenrath Noble / Vindictive Vampire
- Kokusho death trigger (doubled by Teysa = 10 life drain per death)
- Dictate of Erebos locks opponents out of creatures while you sacrifice freely
