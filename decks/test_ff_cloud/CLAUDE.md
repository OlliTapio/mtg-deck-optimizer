# Cloud, Ex-SOLDIER — Naya Equipment Voltron (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Final Fantasy Commander Precon "Limit Break" (FFVII, 2025)

## Commander
Cloud, Ex-SOLDIER (partner with Tifa, Martial Artist)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Naya equipment voltron. Equip Cloud with powerful weapons to reach 7+ power for "Limit Break" triggers. Go tall with one massive creature rather than wide with many.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Ramp (Nature's Lore, Rampant Growth, Sol Ring) and deploy cheap equipment
2. T4: Cast Cloud, equip weapons. Colossus Hammer + Puresteel Paladin is the dream.
3. T5+: Swing with massive equipped Cloud. Darksteel Plate for indestructible, Conqueror's Flail to prevent interaction.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3 lands + ramp + equipment. Cloud is always available.
- KEEP: 2 lands + Sol Ring + any equipment
- MULLIGAN: No equipment at all. No ramp. Color-screwed in 3 colors.

## Win Conditions
- Commander damage with massive equipped Cloud (21 commander damage = kill)
- Hellkite Tyrant stealing all opponents' artifacts
- Sephiroth, Fallen Hero as alternate threat
- Colossus Hammer + free equip = 11+ power Cloud on T4-5
