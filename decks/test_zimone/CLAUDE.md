# Zimone, Mystery Unraveler — Simic Landfall/Manifest (TEST OPPONENT)

## THIS IS A TEST OPPONENT DECK — NOT OWNED
Source: Duskmourn: House of Horror Commander Precon "Jump Scare!" (2024)

## Commander
Zimone, Mystery Unraveler (partner with Kianne, Corrupted Memory)

## Bracket
2 (Core — official precon)

## Theme
Simic landfall and manifest synergy. Ramp aggressively, manifest creatures face-down, then flip them face-up for surprise threats. Big mana into big creatures. Landfall triggers generate additional value.

## Strategy
1. T1-3: Ramp hard (Sakura-Tribe Elder, Rampant Growth, Cultivate, Growth Spiral)
2. T4-5: Cast Zimone, begin manifesting threats. Deploy landfall payoffs (Scute Swarm, Tatyova)
3. T6+: Flip face-down creatures for massive threats (Worldspine Wurm, Hydra Omnivore). Overwhelming Stampede for lethal.

## Mulligan Guide
- KEEP: 3-4 lands + ramp spell + any creature
- KEEP: 2 lands + Sol Ring + ramp + any card
- MULLIGAN: 1 land. No ramp at all. All expensive creatures with nothing to do early.

## Win Conditions
- Overwhelming Stampede with big creatures = lethal swing
- Worldspine Wurm + Biomass Mutation for alpha strike
- Scute Swarm exponential token generation
- Aesi + Tatyova landfall value engine overwhelming opponents

## Known Weakness
- Slow without early ramp — loses to fast aggro
- Board wipes before big creatures stick are devastating
- Manifest is random — sometimes flips lands instead of threats
- Limited interaction (few counterspells, few removal spells)
