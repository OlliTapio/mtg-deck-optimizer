# Yuma, Proud Protector

## Design Principles
- Naya (RGW) lands/landfall/graveyard deck
- Needs lands in graveyard fast to reduce Yuma's casting cost (8 CMC base, -1 per land in GY)
- Desert subtheme: Deserts hitting graveyard create 4/2 Plant Warrior tokens with reach
- Needs haste enablers to close games — tokens and big creatures need to attack immediately
- Budget: no single card over €10

## Known Issues (from initial review)
- Zero board wipes — critical gap
- Only ~7 self-mill sources — need more ways to fill graveyard quickly
- Needs haste to finish games with token army
- Missing instant-speed interaction
- 99 cards (need 1 more)
- 26 maybeboard cards need decisions
