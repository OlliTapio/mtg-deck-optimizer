# Yuma Game Report — 10 LLM Games vs Test Opponents

## Record: 0-10 (0% win rate)
- First eliminated in 8/10 games
- Commander was NEVER cast in any game
- Average elimination turn: 7.3

## Critical Finding
Yuma has a fatal gap from turns 1-5 with no board presence, no blockers, and no interaction. All opponents attack Yuma freely during this window. By the time the engine could start, Yuma is at 10-15 life.

## Worst Matchups
- Edric (evasive swarm + card draw)
- Yuriko (unblockable ninjas)
- Atraxa (flying/vigilance/deathtouch T3-4)
- Krenko (explosive wide tokens)

## MVP Cards
- Dune Chanter (engine starter, but never lived long enough)
- Knight of the White Orchid (rare early play)
- Spelunking (untapped lands = tempo)
- Springbloom Druid (land sac + ramp)

## Underperformers
- Yuma commander (never cast in 10 games)
- Felidar Retreat, Garruk's Uprising, Annie Joins Up (win-more enchantments, dead when losing)
- Hour of Promise (5 mana too slow)
- Overwhelming Stampede (too expensive when behind)

## Recommended Changes
1. Add early blockers/deterrents (CMC 1-3): deathtouch creatures, walls
2. Speed up GY filling: more Harrow-type sac-ramp, more self-mill
3. Cut expensive do-nothing enchantments (Garruk's Uprising, Annie Joins Up, Prismatic Undercurrents)
4. Add more instant-speed interaction (Beast Within, Generous Gift at low CMC)
5. Reduce reliance on commander — need a Plan B that doesn't need 6+ mana and 3 GY lands
6. The deck cannot survive to T5 without early blockers against aggro opponents
