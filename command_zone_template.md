# Command Zone Deck Building Template (New Era, 2025)

Source: The Command Zone Episode 658 (February 2025)

## Card Counts

| Category | Count | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Lands** | 38 | Based on Frank Karsten's hypergeometric analysis for reliable land drops |
| **Ramp** | 10 | Mana acceleration (rocks, dorks, land search) |
| **Card Advantage** | 12 | Draw, impulse draw, card selection |
| **Targeted Disruption** | 12 | Single-target removal, counterspells, interaction |
| **Mass Disruption** | 6 | Board wipes, mass removal |
| **Plan Cards** | ~22 | Your deck's strategy, synergies, win conditions |

Total: 38 + 10 + 12 + 12 + 6 + 22 = 100

## Key Points

- Cards can count in multiple categories (e.g. a removal spell that also draws counts as both disruption and card advantage)
- 2-mana cards should be the most numerous (~18 cards at 2 CMC), tapering off at higher values
- Episode 659 ("The Problem with Deckbuilding Templates") discusses when to deviate based on your specific commander and strategy

## When to Deviate

- Aggressive decks may want fewer lands and more low-CMC threats
- Graveyard decks may count self-mill as card advantage
- Combo decks may need more tutors and fewer interaction pieces
- Token/go-wide decks may need fewer board wipes (they hurt you too)
- Budget or bracket restrictions may shift the balance
