# Commander Bracket System

Announced by the Commander Rules Committee (September 2024), later managed by Wizards of the Coast.

## Bracket 1 — Casual / Introductory
- Precons out of the box
- No tutors, no fast mana, no extra turns
- Low-power commanders and strategies
- Games are slow and battlecruiser-style

## Bracket 2 — Casual+
- Upgraded precons, focused strategies
- Efficient removal and interaction allowed
- No infinite combos
- No mass land destruction
- Some tutors OK (land tutors, creature tutors)
- No free counterspells
- Power level roughly 4-6

## Bracket 3 — Focused / Optimized
- Synergistic, powerful decks
- Combos allowed but should require multiple pieces and board presence
- No instant-win 2-card combos without interaction windows
- Fast mana like Sol Ring, Mana Crypt discouraged but not banned
- Tutors allowed
- Power level roughly 6-8

## Bracket 4 — High Power / cEDH
- Everything goes (within banlist)
- Infinite combos, fast mana, stax, mass land destruction
- Optimized for winning as fast as possible
- Power level 8-10

## Notes
- Brackets are self-reported and conversation-based (rule 0)
- The exact boundaries are intentionally fuzzy — it's a social contract tool
- Some cards are flagged as "bracket defining" (e.g. Mana Crypt, Thassa's Oracle)
- Check with your playgroup for their interpretation
